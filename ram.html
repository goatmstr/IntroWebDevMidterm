RAM

RAM or Random Access Memory is essential for the functioning of every program in your PC whether it be your browser or a video game. RAM is where data is stored for extremely quick access. When games are loaded from your storage, important data about the game state and things such as the world are stored in the RAM. Bigger and faster RAM leads to better performance as well as shorter loading times. Today, the optimal RAM to choose is DDR5 RAM as it is the fastest and most people would reccomend atleast 16 GBs of RAM to handle all gaming tasks.
