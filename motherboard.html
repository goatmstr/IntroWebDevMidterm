Motherboard

Building a PC requires assembling many PC parts but also a circuit board to connect them together so that all the components can communicate with the CPU to function properly. That is the purpose of the motherboard. The motherboard is one of the most important parts of a PC build and should be extra carefully researched when picking one to ensure full compatibility of parts. A good motherboard may not increase gaming performance as much as a GPU or CPU would but will certainly ensure that your PC will ruin smoothly without hardware problems. It is important to make sure the motherboard you are choosing has a CPU socket that is compatible with whichever CPU you picked. This means you cannot switch from an AMD processor to an Intel and vice versa so choose wisely!

Motherboards come in many sizes depending on the size of your PC.
