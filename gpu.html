GPU

The GPU or the Graphics Processing Unit is responsible for rendering everything you see on your screen. From windows on your desktop to high quality graphics in a game, the GPU performs high speed mathematical calcuations to generate the many frames you see on your screen every second. This performance usually comes at a high cost however as the GPU is usually the most expensive or second most expensive part of your PC next to the CPU. A good dedicated GPU is what turns any ordinary PC build into a gaming PC. Like with CPUs, the GPU market is dominated by Intel and AMD as well. Intel with they Geforce RTX GPUs and AMD with their Radeon GPUs. The general consensus is that NVIDIA excels at the super high end performance cards such as the 4090 and AMD excels at delivering much better price to performance ratio.

The 4090 is the most powerful GPU in the world right now with an MSRP at $1600 but you will rarely find it at that price.
